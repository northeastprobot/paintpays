## What an AI Phone Agent Actually Does

An AI voice agent is not a phone tree. It's not "press 1 for a quote." It's a conversational AI that answers your phone like a real person, has a natural back-and-forth dialogue, and can do the actual work of a receptionist.

Here's a real interaction:

> **Homeowner:** "Hi, I'm calling about getting my house painted."
> **AI Agent:** "Hey, thanks for calling [your company]! I can definitely help with that. Are you looking at interior, exterior, or both?"
> **Homeowner:** "Exterior. Three-bedroom ranch."
> **AI Agent:** "Great. And are you in [city], or a surrounding area?"

It qualifies the lead, captures contact info, asks about timeline and budget, and either books them directly into your estimate calendar or passes the info to your CRM so you can follow up first thing in the morning. The homeowner gets a call answered. You get a warm, qualified lead — without picking up the phone.

## How It Integrates With Your Business

The best setups aren't just answering calls — they're connected to your CRM and calendar.

Here's how the full flow works:

1. Homeowner calls after hours
2. AI agent picks up, has a qualifying conversation (2-4 minutes)
3. Lead info is captured and logged automatically to your CRM (DripJobs, Jobber, HubSpot, etc.)
4. If the homeowner wants to book, the agent checks your calendar and schedules the estimate
5. A text confirmation goes to the homeowner. A notification goes to you.
6. You wake up in the morning with tomorrow's estimate already on the books.

No phone tag. No playing catch-up. No leads slipping through because you forgot to call back.
